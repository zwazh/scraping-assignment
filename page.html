Lobbyist Expenses on Public Official(s)

View expenditures made by lobbyists on behalf of a public official, the official’s staff or employee, spouse, or child, or for a group of public officials, as reported by the lobbyist.

Click the tabs, sorted by position of the public official. To view the amounts spent. Select View to the left of official’s name (may need to scroll down and/or disable pop-up blocker) to view details of the expense(s). Results display and are sorted by the lobbyist making the expenditure, to see the Principal associated with the lobbyist’s expenditure, switch to View by Principal mode. Use tabs to view results for official’s employee/staff, spouse or child.

View results for prior years by selecting the year from the drop down menu; enter information requested to do an Advanced Search.
